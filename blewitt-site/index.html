WHO•​WE•​ARE

Blewitt is a contemporary jazz music group based in Rome. Emphasizing original compositions, the trio develops a musical and human synergy through the research of a mixture of styles. Many parts of the piano came from a “classical” attitude, particularly from Skrjabin and Beethoven. These parts are intertwined with modern rhythmics (from Neo-Soul to Mediterranean ethnic music, from metal to traditional jazz).

Blewitt's music benefits from the alternation of both visual (written) and audio-tactile (greater freedom of improvisation and/or extemporization) compositional approaches. Blewitt tries to connect these different worlds rediscovering the etymological value of composition.
